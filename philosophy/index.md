# Philosophy — Conceptions of Reality

The foundational graph: how thinkers have conceived of *what reality fundamentally is*, from Plato's
cave to the quantum measurement problem. Not a timeline — a web of influence, reaction, and
synthesis. Seven of these thinkers ground the [EOS operators](/eos/index.md).
