Averroes (Ibn Rushd) — Purist Aristotelianism

Reality is the eternal Aristotelian cosmos of substances actualized by form, with God as pure act and a single shared material intellect for all humanity.

The model of reality

Averroes reads reality through a purified Aristotle: an eternal cosmos of substances structured by form and matter, moved by God as the eternal first mover and pure actuality. Famously, he held the unicity of the material intellect — a single intellect shared by all humans. He defended the demonstrative rights of philosophy alongside revelation.

Key concept

“The Commentator” on Aristotle; unicity of the (material) intellect; eternity of the world.

Relations

He refines Aristotle through his great commentaries and reacts against al-Ghazali in The Incoherence of the Incoherence. He opposes Avicenna’s emanation and treatment of existence. He influenced Aquinas, who engaged him critically, and spawned the Latin Averroism of Siger of Brabant.
