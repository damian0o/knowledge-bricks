## The model of reality

Averroes reads reality through a purified Aristotle: an **eternal cosmos** of substances structured
by form and matter, moved by God as the eternal first mover and pure actuality. Famously, he held
the **unicity of the material intellect** — a single intellect shared by all humans. He defended the
demonstrative rights of philosophy alongside revelation.

## Key concept

"The Commentator" on Aristotle; unicity of the (material) intellect; eternity of the world.

## Relations

He **refines** [Aristotle](/philosophy/ancient/aristotle.md) through his great commentaries and
**reacts against** [al-Ghazali](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/al-ghazali.md) in *The Incoherence of
the Incoherence*. He **opposes** [Avicenna](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/avicenna.md)'s emanation
and treatment of existence. He **influenced** [Aquinas](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/aquinas.md),
who engaged him critically, and spawned the Latin Averroism of Siger of Brabant.
