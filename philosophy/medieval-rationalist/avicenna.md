## The model of reality

Everything that exists is either **necessary** or **contingent**. There is exactly one **Necessary
Existent** (God), whose essence simply *is* existence; everything else is "possible in itself," its
**essence really distinct from its existence**, receiving being from another. The cosmos proceeds
from the Necessary Existent by a Neoplatonic chain of **emanation** through the intellects.

## Key concept

The essence/existence distinction; the **Necessary Existent** (*wajib al-wujud*); emanation.

## Relations

He **synthesizes** [Aristotle](/philosophy/ancient/aristotle.md) with the emanationism of
[Plotinus](/philosophy/ancient/plotinus.md). His essence/existence distinction decisively
**influenced** [Aquinas](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/aquinas.md) and his account of common
natures **influenced** [Duns Scotus](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/duns-scotus.md). His falsafa was
the principal target of [al-Ghazali](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/al-ghazali.md).
