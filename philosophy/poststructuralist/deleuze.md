## The model of reality
Deleuze inverts the priority of identity over difference: difference is not a gap between
pre-existing things but the productive force from which forms emerge. Reality is a flux of
intensities and multiplicities that continually differentiate, organised into provisional
*assemblages* rather than fixed substances. The system never settles into a global
equilibrium; local stability coexists with global drift. A converged system, on this view, is
a dead one.

## Key concept
Difference and repetition; multiplicity; the rhizome and assemblage; productive difference.

## Relations
Deleuze is **influenced by** [Nietzsche's will to power and eternal return](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/nietzsche.md),
by [Bergson's duration and creative becoming](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/bergson.md),
and by [Spinoza's single expressive substance](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/spinoza.md).

## Grounds
Deleuze grounds **two** parts of EOS. He grounds
[O3_LINGUISTIC_SHIFT](/eos/operators/o3-linguistic-shift.md) — meaning is generated within
shifting fields rather than read off fixed definitions — and he grounds
[EOS System Dynamics](/eos/dynamics.md): an open, non-equilibrium system of continuous
structural mutation, where local stability is maintained while global drift is expected and
healthy.
