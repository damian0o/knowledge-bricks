# Poststructuralist & Contemporary

Thinkers who dissolve the stable subject–object reference and recast reality as networks,
discourses, difference, and deferral. These four directly ground the relational and
destabilizing EOS operators.

## Concepts
* [Bruno Latour — Actor-Network Theory](/philosophy/poststructuralist/latour.md) - reality as a network of human and non-human actors
* [Michel Foucault — Power/Knowledge and the Episteme](/philosophy/poststructuralist/foucault.md) - truth constituted by discourse and power
* [Gilles Deleuze — Difference and Becoming](/philosophy/poststructuralist/deleuze.md) - reality as productive difference in an open system
* [Jacques Derrida — Deconstruction and Différance](/philosophy/poststructuralist/derrida.md) - meaning endlessly deferred, no fixed reference
