## The model of reality

Beneath the sensible world lies a mathematical order: number and ratio are the real principles of
things. Musical harmony, the cosmos ("harmony of the spheres"), and the soul are all expressions of
numerical proportion. Reality is intelligible because it is, at bottom, mathematical structure.

## Key concept

"All is number"; the harmony of the spheres; transmigration of souls (metempsychosis).

## Relations

He **influenced** [Plato](/philosophy/ancient/plato.md) profoundly — the mathematical, eternal
character of the Forms and the kinship of soul with the intelligible owe much to Pythagoreanism — and
through the Platonic tradition **influenced** [Plotinus](/philosophy/ancient/plotinus.md). The
Pythagorean Philolaus carried the number-cosmology forward to the next generation.
