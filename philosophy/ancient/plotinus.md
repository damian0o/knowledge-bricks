## The model of reality

All reality flows from one ineffable source, **the One** (the Good), which is beyond being and
thought. From its superabundance it **emanates** in a descending order: **Intellect** (Nous, the
realm of Forms), then **Soul**, then the sensible, material world at the lowest grade of being. All
things proceed from the One and strive to return to it; matter is the faint, near-nothing edge of
this procession.

## Key concept

**The One**; emanation; the three hypostases (One — Intellect — Soul).

## Relations

He **refines** [Plato](/philosophy/ancient/plato.md) into a systematic metaphysics and **synthesizes**
[Aristotle](/philosophy/ancient/aristotle.md) (Nous as self-thinking thought) with the number-mysticism
of [Pythagoras](/philosophy/ancient/pythagoras.md). He **reacts against** the
[Stoics](/philosophy/ancient/stoics.md)' materialism and against the Gnostics. His hierarchy of being
decisively **influenced** [Augustine](/philosophy/medieval-rationalist/augustine.md) and the whole
medieval Neoplatonic tradition.
