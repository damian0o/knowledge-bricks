# Ancient & Hellenistic Philosophy

The opening of the conversation about reality — from the founding clash of Being versus Becoming to
the late skeptical retreat. These bricks are the deepest substrate of the EOS knowledge base.

## The Being / Becoming axis
* [Parmenides — The Way of Truth](/philosophy/ancient/parmenides.md) - reality is one changeless Being; change is illusion.
* [Heraclitus — Flux and the Logos](/philosophy/ancient/heraclitus.md) - reality is ceaseless flux ordered by the Logos.

## Form, number, and substance
* [Pythagoras — All is Number](/philosophy/ancient/pythagoras.md) - reality is numerical structure and harmony.
* [Plato — Theory of Forms](/philosophy/ancient/plato.md) - true reality is eternal Forms; the senses show shadows.
* [Aristotle — Hylomorphism](/philosophy/ancient/aristotle.md) - reality is form-in-matter, immanent in individual substances.

## Materialists and naturalists
* [Democritus & Leucippus — Atomism](/philosophy/ancient/atomists.md) - reality is atoms moving in void.
* [Epicurus — Atomistic naturalism](/philosophy/ancient/epicurus.md) - atoms and void plus an uncaused swerve.
* [The Stoics — Cosmos governed by Logos](/philosophy/ancient/stoics.md) - reality is a rational material continuum bound by fate.

## Unity and doubt
* [Plotinus — Neoplatonic emanation](/philosophy/ancient/plotinus.md) - all reality emanates from the One.
* [Sextus Empiricus — Pyrrhonian skepticism](/philosophy/ancient/sextus-empiricus.md) - suspend judgment about reality itself.
