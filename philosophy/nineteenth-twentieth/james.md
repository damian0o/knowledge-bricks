## The model of reality
Reality is a "blooming, buzzing" plurality — an ongoing stream of pure experience that is neither purely mental nor purely material. Relations between things are themselves part of experience (radical empiricism). The truth of an idea is not a static correspondence but what it does: what it leads to and what it pays in practice.

## Key concept
Pragmatism and radical empiricism ("pure experience" as the primal stuff).

## Relations
James was **influenced by** and **refines** [Charles Sanders Peirce](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/peirce.md), and drew on the neutral elements of [Ernst Mach](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/mach.md). He **influenced** [Edmund Husserl](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/husserl.md), [Henri Bergson](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/bergson.md), [Alfred North Whitehead](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/whitehead.md), and [Hilary Putnam](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/putnam.md).
