## The model of reality
Material and economic conditions are the basis of reality; social being determines consciousness, not the reverse. History is a real, material dialectic driven by class conflict and the development of the forces of production — Hegel's dialectic "stood on its feet" instead of its head.

## Key concept
Dialectical and historical materialism (the inversion of Hegel's idealist dialectic).

## Relations
Marx both **refines** and **reacts against** [G.W.F. Hegel](/philosophy/nineteenth-twentieth/hegel.md) — keeping the dialectic but grounding it in matter rather than Spirit. He was influenced by, then reacted against, Ludwig Feuerbach's materialism, and developed his system with Friedrich Engels.
