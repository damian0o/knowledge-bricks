# EOS Operators

The five active operators applied to every engineering concept, plus the meta-operator over the system itself.

## Active operators
* [O1_FOUNDATION](/eos/operators/o1-foundation.md) - stabilization: adds an external ground assumption before any transformation.
* [O2_INTERNALIZATION](/eos/operators/o2-internalization.md) - cognitive shift: re-roots a concept in the developer's mental model.
* [O3_LINGUISTIC_SHIFT](/eos/operators/o3-linguistic-shift.md) - semantic: reframes a concept in a domain language game.
* [O4_RELATIONALIZATION](/eos/operators/o4-relationalization.md) - network: recasts a concept as the graph of its relations.
* [O5_DE_REFERENTIALIZATION](/eos/operators/o5-de-referentialization.md) - destabilization: removes a stable reference to surface hidden assumptions.

## Meta
* [O_META](/eos/operators/o-meta.md) - operates on the operator system itself: redundancy detection, graph optimization, pruning.
