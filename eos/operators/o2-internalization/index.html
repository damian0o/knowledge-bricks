EOS Operator

O2_INTERNALIZATION

Cognitive-shift operator that replaces a concept's external ground with the developer's own mental structure.

  • #eos
  • #operator
  • #cognitive_shift

Transformation

O2_INTERNALIZATION : C → C - external_ground + cognitive_structure

A cognitive-shift operator. It removes the externally supplied ground established by O1_FOUNDATION and re-roots the concept in the consumer’s existing mental model, so the concept becomes usable from inside their understanding rather than as an imported authority.

Engineering mapping

Map the concept onto the developer’s existing mental model — their familiar patterns, vocabulary, and architecture style — so a recommendation lands as an extension of what they already know rather than a foreign import.

Philosophical grounding

O2 is not grounded in a philosopher. Its cognitive_structure term is supplied by the User Preference Model (§8) of the ACA agent: the inferred abstraction level, complexity tolerance, and preferred styles of the specific developer. O2 is where the system’s reasoning is personalized to a real human cognitive structure.

Composition rules

Applies after grounding and relational mapping in the default chain O1 ∘ O4 ∘ O2 ∘ O5 ∘ O3. It precedes destabilization so the concept is anchored in the developer’s model before assumptions are challenged.

Relations

  • Consumes the ground established by O1_FOUNDATION.
  • Operates on the relations surfaced by O4_RELATIONALIZATION.
  • Precedes O5_DE_REFERENTIALIZATION in the safe configuration.
