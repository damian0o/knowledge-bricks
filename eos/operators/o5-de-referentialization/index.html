Transformation

O5_DE_REFERENTIALIZATION : C → remove_stable_reference(C)

The most dangerous operator. It strips the fixed reference point from a concept, exposing the assumptions a “stable” design silently depends on. Necessary for genuine expansion of the solution space — destructive without prior grounding.

Engineering mapping

Surface the hidden assumptions embedded in current architectural choices. Treat every “best practice” as the currently dominant discourse rather than truth; challenge the assumed solution and reveal the hidden dependencies a design takes for granted.

Philosophical grounding

Grounded in Foucault: discourses determine what can count as knowledge — every best practice is a power structure defining what is thinkable. Also grounded in Derrida: every sign defers to another, so no concept has a final fixed meaning. Deconstruction is a tool, not a worldview.

Composition rules

Never apply O5 without O1_FOUNDATION first — destabilization without prior grounding causes cognitive rejection and dissolves the ground it stands on (Foucault and Derrida without Kant produce paralysis). O_META prunes unstable transformations where O5 ran ungrounded in the last 3 interactions.

Relations

  • Precedes the reframing of O3_LINGUISTIC_SHIFT.
  • Policed by O_META.
