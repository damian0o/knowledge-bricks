## Statement

Meaning emerges from transformations. A concept *is* what operators do to it — meaning is an effect of the transformation chain, not a property held prior to it.

## What it does NOT mean

It does not mean meaning is arbitrary. The transformations are disciplined and composable; meaning is stable *locally*, within a given configuration, even though the system drifts globally.
