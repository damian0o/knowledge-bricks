EOS Axiom

Concepts have no fixed grounding

Meaning emerges from transformations applied to a concept, not from any inherited definition.

  • #eos
  • #axiom
  • #foundation

Statement

Concepts have no fixed grounding. A concept’s content is not given by an external authority or a final definition; it is whatever operators do to it within a context.

What it does NOT mean

This is not relativism. It means local stability is constructed, not inherited — the agent must build stability through operator composition (starting with O1_FOUNDATION) rather than assume it from outside.
