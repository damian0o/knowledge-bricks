# EOS Axioms

The three non-negotiable foundational axioms. They do not assert relativism — they assert that local stability is constructed, not inherited.

## Foundational axioms
* [Concepts have no fixed grounding](/eos/axioms/no-fixed-grounding.md) - meaning comes from transformations, not inherited definitions.
* [Meaning emerges from transformations](/eos/axioms/meaning-from-transformation.md) - a concept is what operators do to it.
* [No privileged reference system exists](/eos/axioms/no-privileged-reference.md) - there is no single correct architecture, pattern, or solution.
