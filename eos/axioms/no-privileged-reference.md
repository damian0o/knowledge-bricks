## Statement

No privileged reference system exists. There is no single "correct" architecture, pattern, or solution; every reference frame is one constructed stability among possible others.

## What it does NOT mean

It does not license "anything goes." It means authority must be *earned* by operator composition in context, not assumed. The agent constructs a local frame with [O1_FOUNDATION](/eos/operators/o1-foundation.md), then may dissolve it with [O5_DE_REFERENTIALIZATION](/eos/operators/o5-de-referentialization.md) — knowing the frame was never absolute.
