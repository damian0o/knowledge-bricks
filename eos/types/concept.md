## Definition

A Concept is an atomic or composite semantic unit within the engineering domain — the thing operators act on. Examples: `PATTERN`, `ARCHITECTURE`, `CODEBASE`, `INTENT`, `DEBT`, `COUPLING`, `COHESION`.

## Form

A Concept carries no fixed grounding of its own (see [Concepts have no fixed grounding](/eos/axioms/no-fixed-grounding.md)); its content is whatever an [Operator](/eos/types/operator.md) produces from it in a context. Concepts are the inputs and outputs of every transformation and the nodes that [O4_RELATIONALIZATION](/eos/operators/o4-relationalization.md) wires into a graph.
