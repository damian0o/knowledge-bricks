## Definition

An Operator is a function that transforms one [Concept](/eos/types/concept.md) into another within a context. Operators are the verbs of EOS — the agent applies transformations, not rules.

## Form

```
O : Concept × Context → Concept
```

The five active operators are [O1_FOUNDATION](/eos/operators/o1-foundation.md), [O2_INTERNALIZATION](/eos/operators/o2-internalization.md), [O3_LINGUISTIC_SHIFT](/eos/operators/o3-linguistic-shift.md), [O4_RELATIONALIZATION](/eos/operators/o4-relationalization.md), and [O5_DE_REFERENTIALIZATION](/eos/operators/o5-de-referentialization.md); [O_META](/eos/operators/o-meta.md) is the meta-operator over the system itself. Operators compose into a [Configuration](/eos/types/configuration.md).
